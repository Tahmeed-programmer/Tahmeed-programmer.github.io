Expertise

labor

Labor law is a field within civil law. Labor law is the area of law that deals with the rights and obligations of employers and employees in the workplace. It covers issues such as contracts, wages, benefits, health and safety, discrimination, harassment, termination, unions and collective bargaining As a labour lawyer, I can help you with any legal problem related to your work, whether you are an employee, an employer or a union member. I have experience in advising, negotiating and litigating on behalf of my clients in various sectors and industries

Financial

Financial law is the area of law that deals with banking, finance and debt transactions. It covers issues such as lending, borrowing, restructuring, leasing, asset management and compliance. As a financial lawyer, I can help you with any legal problem related to your financial activities, whether you are a bank, a company, an investor or a regulator. I have experience in advising, negotiating and drafting on behalf of my clients in various sectors and industries.

Criminal

Criminal law is the area of law that deals with offences against the state or society. Criminal lawyers may represent the prosecution or the defence in various matters such as murder, assault, theft, fraud, corruption and terrorism. As a criminal lawyer, I can help you with any legal problem related to your criminal charges, whether you are the accused or the victim. I have experience in representing clients in various courts and tribunals

sharia

Sharia law is the application of Islamic religious law to various aspects of public and private life. It is derived from the sacred scriptures of Islam which are the Quran and the Hadith. As a sharia lawyer, I can help you with any legal problem related to your personal, family, business or social affairs, whether you are a Muslim or a non-Muslim. I have experience in advising, negotiating and drafting on behalf of my clients in accordance with sharia principles and value

construction

Construction law is the area of legal practice dealing with matters such as infrastructure, construction and engineering. Construction law involves advising clients on contracts, procurement, planning and financing of projects as well as resolving disputes that arise from construction projects, such as delays, defects, claims and liens.As a construction lawyer, I can help you with any legal problem related to your construction project, whether you are a contractor, subcontractor, consultant, developer or owner.
